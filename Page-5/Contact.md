# Little Information on Me:

I was born in the coastal town of [Udupi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Udupi) in Karnataka, a place known for its rich culture, cuisine, and temple architecture. My father works in the field of finance as a Senior Accounts Payable Specialist, and my mother is a homemaker with a remarkable talent for cooking. I am currently pursuing an Integrated PhD in Physical Sciences at JNCASR, where I focus on applying machine learning in materials science.

Outside of science, one of my biggest passions is coffee. I enjoy exploring about different varieties, cultivation techniques, and brewing methods. I believe no coffee beats a well-made filter coffee, and among all varieties, [Peaberry](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Peaberry) remains my favorite. In my spare time, I enjoy cycling, playing badminton and-of course sleeping. Since childhood, I’ve also loved disassembling electronic gadgets and reassembling them, which nurtured my interest in how things work. I am deeply grateful to my parents, who instilled in me a spirit of curiosity and the habit of questioning from an early age.
